Fried Rice

A great fried rice dish that serves as a base you can be creative with!

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp butter, divided
  • 2 large eggs, whisked
  • 2 medium carrots, peeled and diced
  • 1 white onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • salt and black pepper
  • 4 cups cooked and chilled day-old rice
  • 3 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 3-4 tbsp soy sauce, or more to taste
  • 2 tsp oyster sauce
  • 1/2 tsp toasted sesame oil
  1. Head 1/2 tablespoon of butter in a large saute pan over medium-high heat until
    melted. Add egg, and cook until scrambled, stirring occasionally. Remove egg,
    and transfer to a separate plate.
  2. Add an additional 1 tablespoon butter to the pan and melt. Add carrots, onion,
    peas and garlic, and season with a generous pinch of salt and pepper. Saute
    for 5 minutes or until the onion and carrots are soft. Increase heat to high
    add in the remaining 1 1/2 tablespoons of butter, and stir until melted.
    Immediately add the rice, green onions, soy sauce and oyster sauce, and stir
    until combined. Continue saut√©ing for an additional 3 minutes to fry the rice,
    stirring occasionally. Then add in the eggs and stir to combine. Remove from heat
    and stir in the sesame oil until combined. Taste and season with extra soy sauce, if needed.
  3. Serve immediately, or refrigerate for up to 3 days.

Notes

For those of you who love spice, consider adding chili oil or chili garlic paste. This recipe
should be a great base to add a protein of choice or any other favorite ingredients.
